Welcome To LBSITW CSE Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering/Information Technology, established in 2001, offers B.Tech programs in Computer Science and Engineering and Information Technology, each with an intake of 60 students per branch. Our department is distinguished by its highly qualified and experienced faculty specializing in key areas of computer science and engineering, and information technology. Equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, our labs are designed to prepare students to meet the demands of the present and future.

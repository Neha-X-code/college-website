Welcome To LBSITW CSE Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering/Information Technology, established in 2001, offers B.Tech programs in Computer Science and Engineering and Information Technology, each with an intake of 60 students per branch. Our department is distinguished by its highly qualified and experienced faculty specializing in key areas of computer science and engineering, and information technology. Equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, our labs are designed to prepare students to meet the demands of the present and future.

Our Programs

Explore Our Empowering Programs

4 YEAR B.TECH IN CSE

Our department offers two batches of four-year B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), a dynamic discipline that develops strong analytical skills. With state-of-the-art facilities, our graduates are highly sought after in academia and the corporate sector, with an intake of 120 students in the first year and 6 through lateral entry.

Read More

4 YEAR B.TECH IN IT

Our department offers a four-year B.Tech in Information Technology (IT), emphasizing industry-oriented learning with a focus on practical skills like database management and networking. Our state-of-the-art facilities prepare students for high-demand careers in academia and the corporate sector, with an initial intake of 60 students and 3 through lateral entry.

Read More

2 YEAR M.TECH IN CSE

Our two-year Post Graduate program, "M Tech in Computer Science and Engineering," provides specialized knowledge in computer science. With four semesters, it equips students for roles like software engineers, developers, and more. Graduates can pursue Ph.D. opportunities, and we admit 24 students in the first year.

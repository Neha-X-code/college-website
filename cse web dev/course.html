4 YEAR B.TECH IN CSE

Our department offers two batches of four-year B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), a dynamic discipline that develops strong analytical skills. 4 YEAR B.TECH IN IT

Our department offers a four-year B.Tech in Information Technology (IT), emphasizing industry-oriented learning with a focus on practical skills like database management and networking. Our state-of-the-art facilities prepare students for high-demand careers in academia and the corporate sector, with an initial intake of 60 students and 3 through lateral entry.

2 YEAR M.TECH IN CSE

Our two-year Post Graduate program, "M Tech in Computer Science and Engineering," provides specialized knowledge in computer science.
